Our Story

Building dreams, creating legacies, transforming communities since 2018

Our Beginning

Founded in 2018, Heavens Field Homes emerged from a vision to revolutionize the real estate landscape in Jos, Plateau State and beyond. What started as a small team of passionate professionals has grown into a comprehensive real estate solutions provider.

Our founders recognized the need for innovative approaches to property development, investment, and management that prioritize both profitability and community impact.

Our Team

At the heart of Heavens Field Homes are our experienced professionals who bring diverse expertise in architecture, finance, project management, and real estate law. Each team member shares our commitment to excellence and innovation.

We believe that our strength lies in our collaborative approach, combining individual talents to deliver exceptional results for our clients.

Our Journey

2018
Company Founded

Heavens Field Homes was established with a mission to provide innovative real estate solutions in Jos, Plateau State.

2019
First Major Project

Successfully completed our first residential development project in Wuye District, setting new standards for quality and design.

2020
Digital Transformation

Launched our digital platform and expanded services to include smart building technologies and virtual property tours.

2021
Investment Division

Established our real estate investment division, offering strategic opportunities in prime Jos locations.

2022

Extended our services beyond Jos, partnering with developers across Nigeria for comprehensive project management.

2023
Innovation Leadership

Pioneered sustainable building practices and green architecture initiatives in residential and commercial developments.

2024
Community Impact

2025
Future Forward

Continuing our commitment to innovation, sustainability, and client satisfaction as we shape the future of real estate.
